Reviews

Breaking Bad

A riveting series that keeps getting better and better. While not recommended for the whole family, this show does not fail to keep the viewer biting their nails with tension! 5/5


Mr. Robot

If you want a show that is a little more realistic about the world of hacking, this is it. Following the mentally troubled characters is exciting and gets the viewer to want more.

Parks & Recreation

Using a mockumentary format similar to The Office, Parks and Rec is exceptional at providing great laughs while preserving a heart that is admired within the characters. 4/5
